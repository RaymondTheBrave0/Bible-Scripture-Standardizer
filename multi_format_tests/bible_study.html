Bible Study Notes

Today we explored several key passages:

  • Creation Story: Genesis 1:1 tells us "In the beginning God created..."
  • God's Love: John 3:16 is perhaps the most famous verse
  • The Shepherd Psalm: Psalms 23:1 speaks of God's care
  • Love Chapter: 1 Corinthians 13:4-8 defines love beautifully
  • All Things Work Together: Romans 8:28 gives us hope
  • The Beatitudes: Matthew 5:3-12 shows the way to blessing
  • Great Commission: Matthew 28:19-20 is our calling
  • Healing Promise: Isaiah 53:5 speaks of healing
  • Faith Definition: Hebrews 11:1 defines faith
  • Wisdom Request: James 1:5 tells us to ask for wisdom

These references demonstrate various formats that need standardization.
